Compound annual growth rate (CAGR)

`$$
\Large CAGR = \left( V_{final} \over V_{begin} \right)^{1/t} - 1
$$`

Where:

`$$
\begin{align}
V_{begin} & = \text{beginning value} \\
V_{final} & = \text{final value} \\
t & = \text{time in years}
\end{align}
$$`
